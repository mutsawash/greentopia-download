About Greentopia

Greentopia is your gateway to sustainable living. With this app, you can explore eco-friendly products, learn about plant care, and connect with other environmental enthusiasts.

This app aligns with the United Nations' Climate Change Goals by promoting awareness, encouraging green habits, and helping users reduce their carbon footprint through simple, actionable steps.
